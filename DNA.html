DNA is the ONLY resource you can not buy with Dino Bucks. You can get DNA by winning a battle stage, getting in a card pack, arena showcase, selling dinosaurs , or winning it as one of the random prizes in live arena. You can spend DNA in the dinosaur market. Selling dinosaurs can get back half of the DNA spent.
