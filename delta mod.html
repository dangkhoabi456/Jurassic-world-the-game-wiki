Delta:

  • Common
    • Charge: Increase attack stat by 15%.
    • Vitality: Increase HP by 20%.
    • Endurance: Increase HP of entire team by 15%.
    • Wound: Reduce opposing creature's HP by 20% for the duration of the battle.
  • Rare
    • Invigorate: Increase HP of all allied creatures by 500.
    • Berserk: Increase attack stat by 40% and reduce HP by 25%.
    • Nullify: Disable opposing creature's MOD.
  • Super Rare
    • Steal: Steal opposing creature's MOD. One time use.
    • Bleed: After incoming damage, deal 40% of attack stat at end of owner's turn for 3 turns.
    • Predation: Class advantage bonus is +150% of attack stat instead of +50%.
    • Killer Instinct: Gain 1 action point per defeated creature on opponent's team. Triggers at the start of next turn after the opponent creature is defeated.
    • Armored Hide: Damage from first attack received is reduced by -99%.
    • Terrify: Reduce attack stat of entire opposing team by 45%.
    • Adrenaline Rush: Gain 1 action point per defeated creature on team
  • Legendary
    • Toxin: Deal 50% of attack stat to enemy team at end of owner's turn.
    • Mirror: Replace team's MODs with opposing team's MODs
    • Shuffle: Shuffle all MODs at beginning of battle
    • Splinter: Damage dealt is spread across opposing team
    • Regenerate: Heal all hurt allied creatures by 25% of their MAX HP
    • Protectors: Gain 1 defense point for every defeated ally at the beginning of each turn
    • Survival: Remain at 1 HP after receiving lethal damage
