Creation lab

The creation lab is a building where evolution and fusing happen. Hybrids are made here, and dinosaurs can be evolved. There are two tanks for evolve and hydrib, the first one is free but you have to pay 100 dollars for the second one You need to spend DNA to evolve a creature. You can spend DNA for each trial.
