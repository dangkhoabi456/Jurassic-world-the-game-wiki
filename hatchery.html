The hatchery is the Structure were you hatch a dinosaur. There 5 tubes to hatch the players dinosaurs. The first tube is for free, then the second one costs 10 bills . The third tube costs 25 bills, and the fourth tube costs 50 bills. The last tube is free but you will have to buy the "Grand prenium" to active it
