The Innovation Center contains three different features:

  • Creature Glossary

    • The Creature Glossary allows the player to view all the creatures in the game as well as all individuals of that species in the park. Newly obtained species or individual creatures will have an an exclamation mark next to them. This page can be filtered similar to the Market.

  • MODs Manager

    • The MODs Manager allows the player to view all MODs in the game and read about their effects. MODs are categorized by color and can be sorted by this criteria using the manager's sorting function.

  • My Badges

    • My Badges allows the player to view their Badge progress. There is no sorting function, though completed badges are at the bottom of the list. At the top of the page, the progress towards the next Badge Beacon creature is shown. Currently, there is no way to change the displayed beacon creature.
